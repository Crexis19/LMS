The Beginning

2019

At the beginning of 2019, Dan and Elad began working on Zenner AI, an app that would assist travellers with all kinds of trip related issues.

Late 2019

Just after signing with Atlassian, things started moving forward however Covid hit and put everything on hold.

2020

After moving through the motions of the beggining of Covid-19 and all the lockdowns, work on Zenner AI picked up again and things started moving forward, now with the idea of becoming an insurance company rather than just an AI assistant.

Late 2020

After much consideration, the choice was made to rebrand Zenner AI as Faye Travel Insurance.

2021

With the help of Red Antler, the company was redesigned and work on launching our travel insurance became top priotirty.

Mid 2021
