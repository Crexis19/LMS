The Beginning

2019

Dan and Elad began working on Zenner, their AI companion that would provide real-time travel advice and assistance such as early detection of flight cancellations or delays, assistance when making a tight connection and much more.

2020

While Covid-19 initially put a hold on things, it also allowed Dan and Elad time to work on Zenner and they began to realise that rather than being just an AI companion, Zenner would work best as a Travel Insurance Company.

2021

Zenner underwent a lot of changes, the focus shifted from being a chatbot to becoming a full scale travel insurer that would be there to protect a clients entire trip. After some major work was done, Zenner was rebranded as Faye, the company we know and love today!

2022

Faye officially launched April 6th 2022, each month breaking the previous months records. We reached $8 million in our first seed funding round, were in a bunch of press hits and even won some awards! By the end of the year we had grown our team to 30 strong and counting!

2023

Looking forward into 2023 we are ready for all the amazing moments to come, the records we'll break and all the challenges well get the chance to overcome!
