Meet our Co-Founders Dan & Elad!

How they met

Dan and Elad met 13 years ago, when Dan made aliyah from Australia. He did this specifically to launch their first company Wikistrat, which they launched within 48 hours of him landing!

Wikistrat

Wikistrat was the worlds first crowdsourced consultancy platform, which allowed clients to consult with crowds of subject-matter experts about a variety of matters using real time data.

Success with Wikistrat

They grew Wikistrat from a bootstrapped startup to a profitable enterprise relied on by Fortune 500 companies.
